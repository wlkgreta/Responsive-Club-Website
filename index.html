Rocket Power Action Sports Club

We Are Riders On A Mission

Bringing you opportunities to learn and hone your extreme sports skills! We teach classes, plan activity days, and organize excursions for Skateboarding, Snowboarding, Surfing, Rollerblading, and BMX. Rocket Power Action Sports Club is a free-to-join club, although some classes and excursions may require travel-fees or teacher-fees. Sports equipment not provided. A CPR & First Aid certified leader is present at all meet-ups. You must fill out these forms before participating.
